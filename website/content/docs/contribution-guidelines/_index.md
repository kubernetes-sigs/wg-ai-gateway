Welcome to the AI Gateway Working Group! We are excited about the prospect of you joining our community! The Kubernetes community abides by the [CNCF Code of Conduct](https://github.com/cncf/foundation/blob/main/code-of-conduct.md). Here is an excerpt:

> As contributors and maintainers of this project, and in the interest of fostering an open and welcoming community, we pledge to respect all people who contribute through reporting issues, posting feature requests, updating documentation, submitting pull requests or patches, and other activities.

## Getting Started

We have full documentation on how to get started contributing:

- **[Contributor License Agreement](https://github.com/kubernetes/community/blob/master/CLA.md)** - Kubernetes projects require that you sign a Contributor License Agreement (CLA) before we can accept your pull requests
- **[Kubernetes Contributor Guide](https://www.kubernetes.dev/docs/guide/)** - Main contributor documentation, or you can just jump directly to the [contributing page](https://www.kubernetes.dev/docs/contribute/)
- **[Contributor Cheat Sheet](https://www.kubernetes.dev/docs/contributor-cheatsheet/)** - Common resources for existing developers

## Ways to Contribute

We welcome contributions of all kinds! Here are some ways you can help:

- **Improve Documentation** - Help us make our documentation clearer and more comprehensive
- **Build Prototypes** - Create working implementations and proof-of-concepts
- **Share Use Cases** - Share your AI Gateway use cases and requirements
- **Participate in Discussions** - Join our community meetings and discussions
- **Review and Provide Feedback** - Review community contributions and provide constructive feedback

## How to Submit Contributions

1. **Fork the Repository** - Create a fork of our [GitHub repository](https://github.com/kubernetes-sigs/wg-ai-gateway)
2. **Create a Branch** - Create a branch for your changes
3. **Make Your Changes** - Implement your contribution
4. **Submit a Pull Request** - Open a pull request for review
5. **Address Feedback** - Work with reviewers to address any feedback
