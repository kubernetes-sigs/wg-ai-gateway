This is where proposals for the [AI Gateway Working Group](https://github.com/kubernetes/community/tree/master/wg-ai-gateway) live.

Proposals you find here are meant to eventually find their way to the Kubernetes Special Interest Groups (SIGs) and their sub-projects. Our goal is to build consensus around AI Gateway capabilities before presenting them to the relevant SIGs.
